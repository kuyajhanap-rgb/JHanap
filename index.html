About Me

The person behind the process.

I'm Jovan Hanap, a detail-oriented healthcare operations professional based in the Philippines. With over seven years handling enrollment, eligibility verification, and billing at Optum Global Solutions, I've built my career on one principle: accuracy isn't optional in healthcare.

Before healthcare, I worked in mortgage processing, where I developed a sharp eye for documentation, compliance, and audit-ready records. That cross-industry experience means I bring structure and discipline to every client engagement.

Today, I combine that deep operational expertise with AI tools like Claude and n8n to build smarter, faster workflows — so you get the reliability of a seasoned specialist at the efficiency of modern automation.
